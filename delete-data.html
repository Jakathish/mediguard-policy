Request Data Deletion

If you no longer wish to use MediGuard AI and would like all your stored personal and medical data to be completely deleted, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

1. Open the MediGuard AI app.

2. Navigate to your Profile / Settings.

3. Tap on the Delete Account button.

4. This will instantly and permanently remove your profile, medicines, notifications, and images from our system.

Option 2: Delete via Email Request

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request manual data deletion to comply with Play Store policies.

Please send an email to: support@mediguard.ai

Format your email as follows:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Please include the Email Address and Phone Number registered with your MediGuard AI account so we can locate and remove your data.

We will process your request and securely erase all your data within 7 business days.
