Data Deletion Policy – MediGuard AI

MediGuard AI respects your privacy and gives you full control over your personal and medical data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data and what happens when your data is removed.

1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Medical data (medicines, expiry dates, dosage details)
  • Notifications and reminders
  • Uploaded images (medicines or prescriptions)

3. Option 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

1. Open the MediGuard AI app.

2. Go to Settings / Privacy.

3. Tap Delete Account.

4. Confirm the action.

Result: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

4. Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request manual deletion.

Send your request to: support@mediguard.ai

Email Format:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Include your registered Email ID and Phone Number

We will verify your request and securely delete your data within 7 business days.

5. Data Retention

After deletion, your data is permanently removed from our active systems. Some minimal logs may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes.

6. Security of Deletion

We follow industry-standard security practices to ensure your data is securely deleted and cannot be accessed or recovered.

7. Important Note

Account deletion is irreversible. Once completed, all data will be permanently lost and cannot be restored.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at:
support@mediguard.ai


By using MediGuard AI, you acknowledge and agree to this Data Deletion Policy.
